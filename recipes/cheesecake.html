Strawberry Cheesecake Recipe

Return to Homepage

Description

In the mood for a delicious dessert? This lovely strawberry cheesecake recipe is sure to do the trick! With a graham cracker crumb coated crust and a sweet filling loaded with strawberries, this 12 serving cheesecake is sure to serve as great dessert for the whole family. When stored properly in the refridgerator, it can even be enjoyed for 3 days!

Ingredients

  • For the crust: graham cracker crumbs, butter, white sugar, and cinnamon.
  • For the filling: frozen strawberries, cornstarch, cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and eggs.
